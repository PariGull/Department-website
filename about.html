About Our Department

The Department of Computer Science was established in 2005 to provide strong academic foundations and cutting-edge research opportunities in computing technologies.

Vision

  • To become a leading center of computing excellence
  • To foster global innovation and competitiveness

Mission

  • Deliver high quality education
  • Encourage research and development
  • Develop ethical and professional graduates

Chairperson Message

"Our mission is to equip students with knowledge, analytical thinking, and practical skills to meet challenges in the global IT industry."
